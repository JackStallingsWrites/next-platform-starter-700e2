The Experience

Being seen takes trust. So we build it first.

Here's exactly how it works, said plainly — so that nothing about the day is a surprise, and the only thing left for you to do is be present.

Before anything else

First, we talk.

No good photograph starts with a camera. It starts with a conversation. Before we ever schedule a shoot, we talk through what you want to create, what you're hoping to feel, and where your comfort lines are. I want us genuinely on the same page — not assuming, but sure.

Then we give it room. We plan with enough time to do it well — concept, location, wardrobe, pacing — so that when the day comes, nothing is rushed and everything has been thought through. Good work is unhurried. That's not a luxury; it's the method.

  • We start with a real conversation about your vision and your comfort.
  • We plan with ample time — concept, looks, locations — so the day flows.
  • Nothing is scheduled until the expectations are clear and shared.
  • You're always welcome to bring a trusted friend, partner, or hype person.

Afterward

What happens to your images.

Curated by hand, edited by hand, and never shared without your word.

Curation

The session, distilled

I review and curate every frame, then deliver a gallery of the strongest images through a private folder within a few days. Choosing the few is part of the art.

Editing

By hand, in time

You choose 10–15 favorites for detailed artistic editing. Each is reviewed and edited individually — allow two to three weeks, added to your folder as they're finished.

Approval

Your consent travels

Before anything is shared by either of us, we review the final edits together and confirm what's approved. Everyone in a photograph must consent to it being seen.
